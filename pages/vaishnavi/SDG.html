Traffic Flow and Logistic Management within SEZ

Key Aspects

1. Loading / Unloading Schedules

  • Planned times for cargo loading/unloading within the SEZ.
  • Impact: Poorly managed schedules cause congestion at loading bays or entry points, disrupting traffic flow.

2. Downtime for Goods

This refers to the idle time when goods are waiting for processing, transportation, or customs clearance.

Causes of Downtime:

  • Delayed customs clearance.
  • Inefficient loading/unloading processes.
  • Traffic bottlenecks within the SEZ.

Impact:

  • Increased operational costs.
  • Reduced throughput efficiency.
  • Delivery timeline disruptions.

Relation Between the Aspects

  • Efficient schedules reduce waiting times and lower downtime, ensuring faster goods turnover.
  • Coordination Dependency:
    • Unloading delays can cause backups at gates.
    • Poor synchronization for outbound schedules leads to prolonged idle time.
